Fire Prevention Month

March is called the 'Fire Prevention Month' here in the Philippines. This is an oppurtunity or a way to spread awarness about fire prevention. During this month, we should learn more about what causes fires, how do we prevent it, hazards fire comes with and many more.

What do we do when there is a fire?

When there is a fire, it is important to keep calm, be quick and think strategically. Properly make sure to get yourself and others out safely and orderly without causing a panic. Here are some steps on what to do when there is a fire in your vicinity.

Illustration What to do
Learn how to properly use a fire extinguisher
Call 911
Don't use elavators. Instead, use the stairs to get out of the building.
Incase the door is blocked by smoke or fire, place a wet towel under the door.
Evacuate as quickly as possible.

How do we prevent fires from happening?

There are people who are aware of the basics of fire safety. However, it is still important to freshen up our knowledge on this. Here are some important tips on preventing fire.

Illustration What to do
When you are cooking, don't leave the kitchen unattended.
Store flammable products in the right places.
Make sure wires are properly maintained.
Always have a fire extinguisher ready at all times.
If you have a fireplace, make sure items like papers, towels or anything flammable isn't near it.
